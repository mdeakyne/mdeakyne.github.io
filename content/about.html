About

I'm Matt Deakyne. I work at the intersection of data and storytelling — finding signal in noise and shaping it into something that lands.

I believe the best analysis in the world goes nowhere if it can't be communicated. Data without story is just numbers. Story without data is just opinion. I try to build the bridge between the two.

This site is where I think out loud: about writing, about working with data, about the practice of making things that matter.

Agons

I run personal challenges I call agons — short, concrete commitments to push against my defaults. Cold water. Books. Whatever forces some friction. The name comes from the Greek word for contest or struggle. That's the point.
